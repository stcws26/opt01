About Us

Leading welding specialists with years of experience in construction and industrial projects

Building Excellence Through Precision Welding

SoleTrade Welding is a premier welding service provider based in Sydney, Australia. We specialize in delivering high-quality welding solutions for diverse industries including construction, manufacturing, agriculture, and residential projects.

Our team of certified welders brings expertise in various welding techniques and materials. We pride ourselves on safety, precision, and customer satisfaction. Whether it's a small repair or a large-scale industrial project, we deliver exceptional results every time.

Our Services

Comprehensive welding solutions tailored to meet your specific needs

Mobile Welding at Home

Professional on-site welding services at your residence. We bring our equipment and expertise directly to your home for convenient repairs, modifications, and custom fabrications.

Contract Welding Services

Long-term welding contracts for businesses and industrial facilities. Reliable, scheduled services to meet your ongoing welding needs with dedicated support.

Mobile Welding Unit

Fully equipped mobile welding unit that comes to you anywhere in Sydney. Perfect for emergency repairs, on-site fabrication, and projects that can't be moved.

Residential Areas

Specialized welding services for residential neighborhoods. Gates, fences, railings, structural repairs, and custom metalwork for homes and apartments.

Parks & Industrial Zones

Welding services for public parks, recreational facilities, and industrial zones. Equipment repairs, structural work, and maintenance for public and private facilities.

Remote Welding Sydney

Remote welding consultation and project management services in Sydney. Expert guidance for welding projects, inspections, and quality control from anywhere.

Farms & Ranches

Specialized welding for agricultural equipment, fencing, gates, and farm structures. Keep your operations running smoothly with our reliable farm welding services.

Factories & Industrial

Heavy-duty industrial welding for manufacturing facilities, machinery repairs, structural steel work, and production line maintenance.

Cutting & Grooving

Precision metal cutting and grooving services using advanced equipment. Clean cuts for various metals and materials to your exact specifications.
